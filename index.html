Campuses we have

The college is approved by AICTE New Delhi and is permanently affiliated to JNTU-K and is also accredited by NBA and NAAC with “A”Grade and was given the status of Autonomous in the year 2017 by UGC.

R.E.C

Raghu Engineering College is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), accredited by NBA and permanently affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada. The college established in the year 2001 offers engineering courses in five disciplines.

R.I.T

Raghu Institute of Technology is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), accredited by NBA and affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada. The College established in 2007 offers six engineering courses by a group of eminent personalities in the field of engineering education.

R.C.P

Raghu College of Pharmacy is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated to Andhra University, Visakhapatnam. The College was established in 2007 with the objective to train the students in the field of pharmacy.

Our Facilities

Raghu Institute of Technology fully aware of the importance of having facilities with sound facilities maintenance and management. Tasty and Healthy Food
